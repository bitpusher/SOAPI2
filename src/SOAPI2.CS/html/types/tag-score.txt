    <h1>Type  tag_score </h1>

<h2>Discussion</h2>
<div class="indented">
    
    <p>This type represents a user's statistics within a tag.</p>

    <p>Note that this data is often heavily cached or normalized, and thus methods return it may lag significantly behind
    other methods returning similar data.</p>

</div>
