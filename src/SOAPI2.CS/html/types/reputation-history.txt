    <h1>Type  reputation_history </h1>
</div>

<h2>Discussion</h2>
<div class="indented">
    
    <p>This type describes the events that make up a user's reputation history.</p>

    <p>Certain reputation events are private (and types may be reclassified as policy changes), so totaling a user's reputation history may not match their reported reputation
    unless you have access to their entire reputation history.</p>

    <p>Note that new event types may be added at any time, and existing ones may become defunct.</p>

</div>
