    <h1>Type  shallow_user </h1>
</div>

<h2>Discussion</h2>
<div class="indented">
    
    <p>This type represents a user, but omits many of the fields found on the full <a href="/docs/types/user">User</a> type.</p>

    <p>This type is mostly analogous to the "user card" found on many pages (like the question page) on a Stack Exchange site.</p>

</div>
