    <h1>Type  privilege </h1>
</div>

<h2>Discussion</h2>
<div class="indented">
    
    <p>Represents a privilege a user may have on a Stack Exchange site.</p>

    <p>Applications should be aware of, and be able to deal with, the possibility of new privileges being introduced and old ones being removed.</p>

</div>
