    <h1>Type  tag </h1>
</div>

<h2>Discussion</h2>
<div class="indented">
    
    <p>This type represents a tag on a Stack Exchange site.</p>

    <p>Applications should be prepared for the destruction of tags, though this tends to be a rare event.</p>

</div>
