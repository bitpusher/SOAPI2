    <h1>Type  post </h1>
</div>

<h2>Discussion</h2>
<div class="indented">
    
    <p>This type represents the intersection of the <a href="/docs/types/question">Question</a> and <a href="/docs/types/answer">Answer</a> types.</p>

    <p>It's used in cases where it would be beneficial to mix questions and answers in a response.</p>

</div>
